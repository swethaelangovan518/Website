Conference Overview

Advancing innovation through intelligent technologies and global collaboration

ICSAI 2026 provides an international platform for researchers, engineers, academicians, scientists, and industry professionals to exchange innovative ideas and present cutting-edge research in Artificial Intelligence and Smart Technologies.

The conference encourages interdisciplinary collaboration and welcomes original research papers addressing theoretical, experimental, and practical applications of AI systems.

Accepted papers will be published in internationally indexed proceedings and presented during the conference sessions.

Open to all researchers: We welcome submissions from graduate students, postdoctoral researchers, faculty members, and industry practitioners worldwide.

Submission Guidelines

Important information for authors and researchers

Original Research

Submitted papers must present original and unpublished work that is not currently under review in another conference or journal.

Peer Review Process

All submitted papers will undergo a rigorous peer-review process conducted by the international technical committee.

Presentation Format

Accepted authors are expected to present their papers during the conference either physically or through virtual presentation sessions.

Language

All submissions must be written in English and formatted using the IEEE conference template.

Page Limit

Full papers: 6–8 pages. Short/poster papers: 4–5 pages including figures and references.

Publication

Accepted papers will be submitted for inclusion in IEEE Xplore and Scopus indexed proceedings.
