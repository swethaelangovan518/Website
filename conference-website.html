Overview

About ICSAI

The 2026 International Conference on Smart Artificial Intelligence & Innovation (ICSAI 2026) serves as a premier global forum for researchers, engineers, and practitioners to share cutting-edge advances in artificial intelligence, machine learning, and emerging smart technologies.

ICSAI 2026 invites contributions from academia and industry worldwide to foster meaningful discourse, collaborative research, and knowledge exchange at the intersection of intelligence and innovation. The conference welcomes original manuscripts that advance the state of the art in both theoretical foundations and practical applications.

Scope

Topics of Interest

Topics of interest for submission include, but are not limited to:

🤖

Foundations of Artificial Intelligence

AI algorithms, machine reasoning, autonomous agents, computational intelligence, knowledge representation and planning.

🗣️

Natural Language Processing & Generative AI

Large language models, ChatGPT-style systems, NLP pipelines, sentiment analysis, machine translation, and human-computer dialogue.

👁️

Computer Vision & Visual Intelligence

Image understanding, video analysis, object detection, 3D vision, multimedia retrieval, and digital image processing.

📊

Machine Learning & Data Mining

Deep learning, reinforcement learning, federated learning, transfer learning, pattern recognition, and decision intelligence.

🗄️

Big Data Management & Security

Big data architecture, cloud analytics, blockchain for trusted computing, privacy-preserving AI, and data governance.

⚙️

Algorithm Optimization & Theoretical Foundations

Optimization methods, graph algorithms, edge computing, theoretical complexity, and distributed algorithm design.

🏥

AI in Healthcare & Life Sciences

Medical image analysis, drug discovery AI, clinical decision support, wearable intelligence, and genomics data mining.

🚗

Smart Systems & Emerging Technologies

Autonomous vehicles, smart cities, IoT intelligence, robotics, digital twins, AR/VR, and next-generation edge AI.
