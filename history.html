Inaugural Edition

The inaugural ICSAI conference launched fully online, establishing the conference's core values of rigorous peer review, open academic dialogue, and IEEE-quality publishing. The proceedings were indexed in IEEE Xplore from the very first edition, setting a high standard for all future years.

Founding Achievement

ICSAI 2021 achieved full IEEE Xplore indexing from its inaugural edition — a milestone that established the conference's academic credibility from day one.
