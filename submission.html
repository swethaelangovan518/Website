Welcome

Welcome to ICSAI 2026!

The 2026 International Conference on Smart Artificial Intelligence & Innovation (ICSAI 2026) will be held on June 18–20, 2026 in Nanjing, China.

ICSAI 2026 brings together leading academic scientists, researchers, and scholars to exchange and share experiences and research results on all aspects of artificial intelligence, smart systems, and emerging technologies in computer engineering.

The conference provides a premier interdisciplinary platform for researchers, practitioners, and educators to present the most recent innovations, trends, and solutions in the field.

Author Guide

Paper Submission

Follow the steps below to prepare and submit your manuscript to ICSAI 2026.

📋 Submission Steps

01

Prepare Your Manuscript

Use the official IEEE / Springer / ACM template. Maximum 8 pages including references. Ensure originality and proper citations.

02

Check Submission Requirements

PDF format only. All authors must be listed. Abstract: max 250 words. At least 5 keywords required.

03

Submit via Online Portal

Log in or register at EasyChair / CMT. Upload your PDF and fill in all metadata fields.

04

Await Review & Decision

Each paper reviewed by 2–3 expert reviewers. Notification of acceptance/rejection emailed by May 25, 2026.

05

Camera-Ready & Registration

After acceptance, submit camera-ready version and complete registration. At least one author must register and present.

📁 Submission Types

📄

Full Paper

6–8 pages. Original research with complete results.

📝

Short Paper

4–5 pages. Work-in-progress or preliminary results.

🗂️

Poster

2–3 page abstract + poster. Visual presentation.

📰

Journal Track

Extended papers invited to special journal issues.
